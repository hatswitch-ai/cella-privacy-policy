Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 28, 2026

At Cella, we are fundamentally a privacy-first company. We believe that your data belongs entirely to you. This Privacy Policy outlines our strict commitment to protecting your personal information and explains how we handle the minimal data we collect while you use our applications.

1. Data Collection & Our Privacy-First Approach

We are deliberate about data minimization. We collect only the bare minimum Personally Identifiable Information (PII) required to provide and improve our services.

  • Strict Internal Controls: We maintain strict, robust controls regarding data access within our organization. Only authorized systems and personnel have access to data as strictly required for their role.
  • No Selling of Data: We do not, and will absolutely never, share, rent, or sell any customer information to third parties for financial gain or marketing purposes.

2. Device Camera Access (Android & iOS)

Some of our mobile applications require access to your device's camera to enable core functionality. When you grant camera permissions on your Android or iOS device, please be aware of the following:

  • Explicit Use Only: Camera access is used exclusively to provide features within the app (such as scanning barcodes, capturing images for your inventory, etc.).
  • No Background Monitoring: We do not secretly record, monitor, or capture video/images in the background. Camera access is localized to your active session.
  • User Control: You maintain full control over this permission. You can revoke camera access at any time through your device's operating system settings without affecting non-camera features of the apps.

3. Third-Party Service Providers

To deliver a highly reliable, intelligent, and secure experience, we partner with industry-leading third-party service providers. While we utilize external infrastructure, we keep the data shared to the absolute minimum required for functionality. Our generic categories of providers include:

  • Authentication & Account Management: We utilize specialized third-party authentication services to securely manage user identities, logins, and session tokens. This ensures your credentials are encrypted and managed safely without us having to store passwords directly.
  • Cloud Hosting & Data Storage: Our application infrastructure and databases run on compliant, encrypted cloud hosting and database platforms.
  • Artificial Intelligence (AI): We leverage advanced third-party AI frameworks and models to power intelligent features. Any data processed by these AI services is strictly scoped to the immediate request. We do not permit these third-party AI providers to use our user data to train their public or global models.

4. Your Control Over Personal Data

Because it is your data, you possess total control over it. You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your personal information directly within your account settings.
  • Request the deletion of your account and all associated personal data from our active systems.
  • Understand the specific pieces of data we hold about you.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify users of any material changes via the application or email.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please reach out to our privacy team through our application's support channels or via the contact email provided in your account dashboard.
